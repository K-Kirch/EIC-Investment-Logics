● All 15 rules are confirmed. Here is the full set:

  ---
  Document Management

  1. Never delete. Entries are permanent; corrections go in addenda.
  2. Entries are numbered in the order raised, not document order.
  3. Every entry follows the standard template (from [011] onward).
  4. Unresolved questions are flagged [?] and listed in the UQI.
  5. Cross-references between related entries are noted explicitly.
  6. One passage = one entry. Boundary = paragraph break. Bullet lists with an
  introductory sentence = one passage. Standalone headings/single-sentence
  fragments may be combined with an adjacent passage. Full paragraphs separated
  by paragraph breaks cannot be combined.

  Analytical Standards

  7. Logic must organise the passage, not merely appear. The decisive question:
  is this logic shaping what the passage does, or is it merely the topic being
  discussed?
  8. Primary coding threshold is conservative — when in doubt, do not code.
  Undertones and vocabulary-level signals are noted as observations, not
  codings.
  9. Purely procedural passages receive no coding (administrative mechanics,
  deadlines, eligibility lists without rationale).
  10. LOGIC_TENSION only when conflict is surface-visible in the text itself —
  stated, juxtaposed, or structurally legible. Logics presented as complementary
   without acknowledged conflict = Pattern A.
  11. LOGIC_SCIENCE requires scientific governance to be operative (peer review,
   researcher-led agenda, scientific merit as decisive criterion). Science as
  subject matter does not qualify.
  12. TRL as professional gatekeeping = LOGIC_PROFESSION; as operational metric
  or scope-definitional parenthetical = below threshold.
  13. State in purely administrative capacity (allocating budget, administering
  grants) does not rise to LOGIC_STATE. Coded when acting as regulator,
  ecosystem architect, social policy enforcer, or geopolitical actor with stated
   rationale.
  14. Eligibility criteria = procedural unless the rationale is explicitly
  geopolitical or security-driven and stated in the text.
  15. Vocabulary does not determine logic. The organising framework determines
  the coding.

  ▎ "Innovative ideas put forward under this Challenge must explore novel

  traditional, deep learning and reinforcement learning paradigms. These could 
  be inspired by developments in diverse fields such as neuroscience, biology, 
  physics, philosophy and more.

  ▎ The proposals should address one or more of the following cognitive 
  capabilities:

  ▎ 1. Deep Reasoning: Moving beyond statistical pattern matching to support 
  causal inference, logical reasoning, and context-aware or commonsense 
  decision-making in complex, unstructured environments. This requires shifting 
  from purely data-driven correlations to AI systems capable of understanding 
  why patterns emerge, identifying underlying causes, and drawing valid 
  conclusions through both deductive and inductive processes. Neuro-symbolic 
  approaches, which combine the learning power of neural networks with the 
  structured inference of symbolic reasoning are particularly encouraged to 
  advance these capabilities. Integrating contextual and commonsense knowledge 
  enables AI to interpret information more holistically, adapt decisions 
  dynamically, and handle ambiguity and uncertainty. Deep reasoning systems 
  should be able to reconcile multiple sources of information, provide 
  transparent and explainable rationales for their outputs, and align with human
   values and expectations, ensuring trustworthy and accountable operations in 
  demanding real-world scenarios."

  ---
  Analysis:

  The introductory sentences set the methodological expectation: proposals must
  explore novel approaches beyond current paradigms, with cross-disciplinary
  inspiration explicitly invited. The modal "must" is a directive — this is not
  describing what researchers might do, but prescribing what the programme
  requires.

  Area 1 then specifies what Deep Reasoning entails at a technical level: causal
   inference, logical reasoning, commonsense decision-making, deductive and
  inductive processes. Neuro-symbolic approaches are "particularly encouraged."
  The passage closes by invoking alignment with human values, transparency, and
  trustworthiness.

  The organising logic here is LOGIC_SCIENCE: the passage governs what counts as
   valid scientific approach (novel frameworks, cross-disciplinary inspiration,
  specific cognitive capabilities), defines the research agenda, and treats
  scientific capability advancement as the evaluative frame. The directive "must
   explore novel approaches" is the programme setting the scientific agenda —
  researcher-led in framing, even if institutionally prescribed.

  "Align with human values and expectations, ensuring trustworthy and
  accountable operations" introduces a normative register that could touch
  LOGIC_STATE (trustworthiness, accountability as regulatory/societal
  requirements). However, in this passage it reads as a scientific design
  criterion — what Deep Reasoning systems technically should do — rather than a
  state mandate being enacted. The EU AI Act is not cited here (it appears later
   in Expected Outcomes).

  Proposed coding: LOGIC_SCIENCE
