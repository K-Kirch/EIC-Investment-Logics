# Institutional Logics — Quick Reference Matrix
## EIC Pathfinder & STEP Scale Up

---

| | **LOGIC_SCIENCE** | **LOGIC_MARKET** | **LOGIC_STATE** | **LOGIC_PROFESSION** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Central question** | Is this excellent science? | Will this create and capture market value? | Does this serve European strategic interests? | Does this meet professional standards? |
| **Source of authority** | Scientific expertise and peer judgment | Investors and market signals | Legislative mandate and public institutions | Credentialled practitioners and professional bodies |
| **Measure of success** | Novelty, rigour, frontier contribution | Commercial viability, growth, return | Sovereignty, security, geopolitical positioning | Conformity with professional norms and expert endorsement |
| **Who decides** | Peer reviewers and scientific jury | Co-investors and market demand | EU institutions and the STEP Regulation | External specialists (IP, technology, investment) |
| **Typical vocabulary** | "scientific basis", "proof of principle", "frontier", "novel", "interdisciplinary", "TRL 1–4" | "market opportunity", "commercial potential", "funding round", "investor", "scale", "revenue", "first come first served" | "strategic dependencies", "sovereignty", "European interests", "protect", "strategic autonomy", "geopolitical" | "due diligence", "expert", "leading specialists", "Freedom to Operate", "credible plan", "assessment" |
| **Instrument it favours** | Open grant, peer-reviewed call | Equity, co-investment, competitive pitch | Regulated mandate, conditioned award, IP clause | Expert-validated selection, professional certification |
| **Expected applicant** | Rigorous researcher with an ambitious scientific vision | Commercially viable company with a credible growth plan | Strategic asset with European anchoring | Professionally organised entity able to speak expert languages |
| **Pathfinder signal** | Primary logic in Pathfinder Open | Secondary — commercialisation pathway required | Tertiary — visible in Pathfinder Challenges | Present in jury evaluation and expert review |
| **STEP signal** | Residual — "deep tech in nature" excellence criterion | Primary in instrument design (equity, rounds, co-investment) | Primary in programme purpose (sovereignty, strategic tech) | Primary in due diligence and evaluation architecture |

## Logic Tension — When to Add `LOGIC_TENSION`

Add `LOGIC_TENSION` alongside the relevant logic codes when a passage visibly tries to serve two logics at once and the text shows the strain.

| Tension pair | What it looks like |
|---|---|
| SCIENCE ↔ STATE | Scientific merit criterion applied within a state-defined thematic scope; researcher autonomy constrained by strategic priorities |
| MARKET ↔ STATE | Market instrument (equity) used to achieve state goal (sovereignty, IP retention); commercial logic form, geopolitical logic content |
| SCIENCE ↔ MARKET | Commercialisation pathway required from TRL 1–4 research; impact criteria imposed on basic science |
| STATE ↔ PROFESSION | Geopolitical priority set by regulation but validated by expert due diligence; tension between political authority and expert authority |
