Logic Tension — When to Add LOGIC_TENSION

Add LOGIC_TENSION alongside the relevant logic codes when a passage visibly tries to serve two logics at once and the text shows the strain.

Tension pair What it looks like
SCIENCE ↔ STATE Scientific merit criterion applied within a state-defined thematic scope; researcher autonomy constrained by strategic priorities
MARKET ↔ STATE Market instrument (equity) used to achieve state goal (sovereignty, IP retention); commercial logic form, geopolitical logic content
SCIENCE ↔ MARKET Commercialisation pathway required from TRL 1–4 research; impact criteria imposed on basic science
STATE ↔ PROFESSION Geopolitical priority set by regulation but validated by expert due diligence; tension between political authority and expert authority

Basis: Friedland & Alford (1991); Thornton, Ocasio & Lounsbury (2012)
